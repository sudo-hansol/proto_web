Learning Objectives

01

Mathematical Foundation

Provide solid foundation in pure and applied mathematics including analysis, algebra, probability, and differential equations.

02

AI & Data Science Expertise

Develop expertise in artificial intelligence and data science including machine learning, deep learning, NLP, and computer vision.

03

Computational Skills

Equip students with computational skills in Python for implementing mathematical models and AI algorithms.

04

Interdisciplinary Competence

Foster interdisciplinary competencies including scientific communication, ethical AI practices, and research methodology.

Semester Modules

Click on each semester to view detailed course modules

Semester 1

Foundation

Analysis I

Fundamental concepts of mathematical analysis including limits, continuity, and differentiation.

Algebra I

Introduction to algebraic structures, linear equations, and matrix operations.

Algorithms and Data Structures 1

Basic algorithms, data structures, and computational thinking fundamentals.

Discrete Mathematics

Mathematical structures that are fundamentally discrete rather than continuous.

Free/Open Source Software

Introduction to open-source software development and collaborative tools.

Introduction to Data Visualization

Principles and techniques for effective data visualization and graphical representation.

Academic English for AI & Data Science

English language skills specialized for academic and technical communication in AI fields.

Semester 2

Intermediate

Analysis II

Advanced topics in analysis including integration, sequences, and series.

Algebra II

Vector spaces, linear transformations, and eigenvalues.

Algorithms and Data Structures 2

Advanced algorithms, complexity analysis, and optimization techniques.

Probability and Descriptive Statistics

Fundamental probability theory and statistical methods for data description.

Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

Basic concepts, history, and fundamental techniques in artificial intelligence.

Mathematical Physics Equations

Mathematical modeling of physical phenomena using differential equations.

Scientific Computing with Python

Python programming for scientific computing and mathematical applications.

Semesters 3-6

Advanced

Advanced topics covering machine learning, deep learning, NLP, computer vision, and specialized mathematics. See the original about page for complete details.
