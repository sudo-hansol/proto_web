Introduction to the Major

Applied Mathematics & AI for Data Science is a three-year undergraduate program at University of Kasdi Merbah Ouargla, Faculty of Mathematics and Matter Sciences. The program integrates fundamental mathematics, applied mathematics, computer science, and data science with strong emphasis on artificial intelligence applications.

Program Structure

  • Duration: 6 semesters (3 years)
  • Total Credits: 180 credits (30 per semester)
  • Weekly Volume: 504 hours total per semester (36h weekly)
  • Teaching Units: Fundamental (60.55%), Methodological (31.11%), Discovery (5.56%), Transversal (2.78%)

Learning Objectives

  • Provide solid foundation in pure and applied mathematics (analysis, algebra, probability, differential equations)
  • Develop expertise in AI and data science (machine learning, deep learning, NLP, computer vision)
  • Equip students with computational skills in Python for implementing mathematical models
  • Foster interdisciplinary competencies including scientific communication and ethical AI practices

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as:

  • Data Scientists: In energy sector (Sonatrach), technology companies, finance
  • AI Engineers: Machine learning specialists, NLP engineers, computer vision experts
  • Research Analysts: In academic institutions, research centers
  • Industry Roles: In tech-driven industries across Algeria (Algiers, Oran) and internationally
  • Public Sector: Government agencies for data-driven policy analysis

Further Studies

Students may pursue:

  • Master's Programs: AI, Data Science, Computational Mathematics, Applied Mathematics
  • Research Fields: Machine Learning, NLP, Computer Vision, Stochastic Processes
  • Interdisciplinary: Bioinformatics, Financial Mathematics, Engineering
  • Professional Certifications: TensorFlow Developer, AWS Certified Data Analytics
