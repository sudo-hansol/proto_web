Introduction to the Major

Applied Mathematics & AI focuses on using mathematics to solve real-world problems in AI and Data Science. Students gain strong foundations in linear algebra, probability, statistics, algorithms, and AI projects.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as Data Scientists, AI Engineers, Machine Learning Specialists, Research Analysts, or in tech-driven industries.

Further Studies

Students may pursue Master's programs in AI, Data Science, Computational Mathematics, or research in Machine Learning, NLP, and Computer Vision.
