Frequently Asked Questions

Where does my submission go?

Your submission is sent directly to the administrator's email (khaldiislam720@gmail.com) for review. Once approved, it will be added to the resources database.

How long does review take?

Resources are typically reviewed within 24-48 hours. If you provided an email, you'll receive a notification once your submission is approved.

Can I submit anonymously?

Yes! You can leave the name and email fields blank to submit anonymously. Only the resource details and link will be sent.

What if my resource gets rejected?

If you provided an email, we'll notify you with the reason for rejection and suggestions for improvement. Common issues include broken links, copyright violations, or poor quality.
