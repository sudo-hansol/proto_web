Mathematics Applied for Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

Introduction to the Specialization

The Licence Académique in Mathématiques Appliquées pour l'Intelligence Artificielle et la Science des Données (Applied Mathematics for Artificial Intelligence and Data Science) is a three-year undergraduate program structured over six semesters, with a total of 180 credits (30 credits per semester).

Program Overview

  • Institution: University of Kasdi Merbah Ouargla
  • Faculty: Faculty of Mathematics and Material Sciences
  • Department: Mathematics
  • Program Type: Double Competence Academic License
  • Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)
  • Total Credits: 180 ECTS

Program Objectives

  • Provide a solid foundation in pure and applied mathematics (analysis, algebra, probability, differential equations)
  • Develop expertise in artificial intelligence and data science (machine learning, deep learning, NLP, computer vision)
  • Equip students with computational skills in Python and other tools for implementing mathematical models and AI algorithms
  • Foster interdisciplinary competencies including scientific communication and ethical considerations in AI
  • Prepare graduates for professional roles in data science, AI, and related fields or for further studies

Google Drive Integration

How to Add Google Drive Files:

  1. Upload your file to Google Drive
  2. Right-click on the file and select "Get link"
  3. Change sharing settings to "Anyone with the link can view"
  4. Copy the file ID from the URL (the part after /d/ and before /view)
  5. Replace "YOUR_FILE_ID" in the script.js file with your actual file ID

Example: https://drive.google.com/file/d/FILE_ID_HERE/view?usp=sharing

Sharing Settings

Make sure your file is set to "Anyone with the link can view" for students to access it.
